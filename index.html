About Me

Passionate about

I'm Anamika Pawar, a Literature student with a deep love for the written word. From Victorian novels to contemporary poetry, I find beauty and meaning in every page.

My work spans literary analysis, creative writing, and cultural criticism. I believe literature is a mirror held up to society - reflecting who we are and who we could be.

Currently pursuing my degree while writing essays, short fiction, and contributing to the college literary magazine.
